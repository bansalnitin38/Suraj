Submersible pumps are found in many applications: Single stage pumps are used for borewell pump. Multiple stage submersible pumps are typically lowered down a borehole and most typically used for residential, commercial, industrial water extraction (abstraction), municipal, oil wells and in water wells Other uses for submersible pumps include sewage treatment plants, seawater handling, fire fighting (since it has flame retardant cable), water well and deep well drilling, offshore drilling rigs, artificial lifts, mine dewatering, and irrigation systems. Pumps in electrical hazardous locations used for combustible liquids or for water that may be contaminated with combustible liquids must be designed not to ignite the liquid or vapours.
